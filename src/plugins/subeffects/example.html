Apply style to objects only when certain substep is active

<div class="row">
    <div class="column" style="font-size: 0.65em;">
        <ul>
            <li class="substep obj1" data-style-only-obj5="opacity: 0;" 
                data-style-only-obj1="transition: color 1s linear; color: yellow;">
                Step 1: step 1 turns yellow and hide the figure</li>
            <li class="substep obj2" data-style-only-obj1="transition: color 1s linear; color: red;" 
                data-style-only-obj2="border: 2px solid white;">Step 2: step 1 turns red and step 2 adds border</li>
            <li class="substep obj3" 
                data-style-only-obj3="transition: opacity 1s linear, color 1s linear; opacity: 50%; color: yellow;">
                Step 3: step 3 turn yellow and trasparent</li>
            <li class="substep obj4" data-style-only-obj1="border: 2px solid green;" 
                data-style-only-obj4="transform: translate(50px,0px);">Step 4: step 1 adds border and step 4 moves</li>
        </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="column">
        <div class="img5" data-style-base="width:100%; height: 200px; background: red;"></div>
    </div>
</div>
Use 'data-style-only-CLASS' to add properties to objects with class=CLASS using substeps:
  • Step 1: step 1 turns yellow and hide the figure
  • Step 2: step 1 turns red and step 2 adds border
  • Step 3: step 3 turn yellow and trasparent
  • Step 4: step 1 adds border and step 4 moves
